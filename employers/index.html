Careers opportunities

Our students are diverse, experienced, hardworking, and academically talented. Our CUNY2x and CIE programs can serve as your first destination to recruit the most talented and diverse candidates.

Request a Customized Recruiting Strategy

Our Industry and Student Connections sepecialists are eager to connect recruiters with engineering and computer majors students and alumni. We want all employers to be successful in their recruiting endeavors that’s why we offer:

  • Host an Information Session that allows you to meet and build connections with high-caliber students.
  • Recruiting Programs Phone Support
  • Navigating Academic Departments Through Staff Liaisons
  • Assistance with Developing an Internship Program
  • Utilize LaGuardia Handshake for Your Recruiting Needs
  • Company Site Visit: We can bring a group of select students to your facility for a tour, presentation on available careers, and networking opportunities.
